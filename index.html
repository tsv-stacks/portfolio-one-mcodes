About Me

A Microbiologist with focus on the shelf-life and spoilage of food and method validation.

Experience of working within the fast paced and exciting environment of Research & Development at a major confectionary and chocolate company where I helped in the development and launch of many different Cadbury Dairy Milk and Milka products.

I began my learning journey with FreeCodeCamp and the Odin Project before deciding coding was something I was genuinely interested in and enjoyed the creativity (as well as the challenges!) to pursue a career in.

After spending many years modding games and old consoles, I became more and more interested in learning how these programs and modification files were made.

On my learning journey (or rabbit hole) this led me to independently developed (indie) games and it amazed me how small groups of people or individuals were able to develop these games that would keep me occupied for hours. This is what sparked my interest in coding.

I am currently a student at Manchester Codes learning Full Stack Web Development and ultimately looking to become a Junior Developer to begin my career change!
